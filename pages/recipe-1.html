Recipe 1

Description

This is the first recipe. It is delicious and easy to make!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 cup of butter

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix the flour, sugar, and butter until well combined.
  3. Press the mixture into a greased baking dish.
  4. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until golden brown.
