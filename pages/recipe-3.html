Recipe 3

Description

This is the third recipe. It is delicious and easy to make!

Ingredients:

  • 12 zamburiñas
  • 2 dientes de ajo pelados muy picados
  • 4 cucharadas de aceite de oliva
  • perejil fresco.

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place the scallops in a baking dish.
  3. In a small bowl, mix the garlic and olive oil.
  4. Drizzle the garlic oil over the scallops.
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the scallops are cooked through and slightly golden on top.
  6. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.
