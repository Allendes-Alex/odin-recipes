Recipe 2

Description

This is the second recipe. It is delicious and easy to make!

Ingredients:

  • Gamba o gambón 250 g
  • 2 Dientes de ajo
  • 1 Guindilla de Cayena
  • 1 Cucharada de aceite de oliva
  • Sal

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the shrimp and cook until they turn pink and are done, about 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add the garlic and chili pepper. Cook for 1 minute.
  4. Season with salt to taste.
