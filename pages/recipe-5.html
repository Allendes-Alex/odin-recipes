Recipe 5

Description

This is the second recipe. It is delicious and easy to make!

Ingredients:

  • camarones 250 g
  • Pasta integral
  • aceite de oliva
  • sal a gusto

Instructions

  1. Cook the whole wheat pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Add the shrimp and cook until they turn pink and are cooked through, about 2-3 minutes per side.
  4. Season with salt to taste.
  5. Toss the cooked pasta with the shrimp in the skillet until well combined and heated through.
